About Atelier

Our Story

Atelier was founded on a simple belief: that the clothes we wear should be as timeless as they are beautiful, as sustainable as they are comfortable. In a world of fast fashion and disposable trends, we stand for something different.

We began our journey in 2015, working with a handful of artisan mills in Europe and Asia. Today, we partner with over thirty traditional weavers and producers who share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and fair labor practices.

Our Values

Every piece in our collection tells a story. From the organic cotton fields of Turkey to the silk workshops of Como, Italy, we trace each fabric back to its source. We believe in transparency, which is why we share the origin of every material we use.

Natural fibers are not just better for the environment—they're better for you. Cotton breathes, silk regulates temperature, linen becomes softer with age, and wool resists odors naturally. These are fabrics that have dressed humanity for thousands of years, and they will continue to do so long after synthetic alternatives have faded.

Craftsmanship

We work exclusively with mills and ateliers that prioritize traditional techniques. Our silk scarves are hand-rolled in Como using methods passed down through generations. Our wool coats are cut and tailored by craftspeople who have honed their skills over decades.

This attention to detail means our garments last longer, wear better, and develop character over time. We design for durability, not disposability.

Looking Forward

As we grow, our commitment remains unchanged: to create beautiful, lasting garments from the world's finest natural fibers. We're constantly exploring new partnerships, new techniques, and new ways to reduce our environmental impact while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Thank you for being part of our story.
