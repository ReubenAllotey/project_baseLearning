Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't quite right, we offer hassle-free returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Return Policy

You may return any unworn, unwashed item with original tags attached within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition with no signs of wear, alterations, or damage.

All returns are subject to inspection. If an item is returned in unsatisfactory condition, we reserve the right to refuse the return or issue a partial refund.

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability. If you'd like to exchange an item, please contact our customer service team at exchanges@atelier-fabrics.com with your order number and the item you'd like to receive instead.

We'll send your replacement as soon as we receive your original item back in acceptable condition. There's no charge for exchanges on items of equal value.

How to Return

Step 1: Contact our customer service team at returns@atelier-fabrics.com with your order number and the item(s) you wish to return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

Step 2: Pack your item(s) securely in the original packaging if possible, or any suitable box. Include the return form we provided with your order, or a note with your order number and reason for return.

Step 3: Affix the prepaid return label to your package and drop it off at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive a tracking number for your records.

Step 4: Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for all domestic returns. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs, though we'll gladly reimburse you up to $15 for documented shipping expenses on approved returns.

We recommend using a tracked shipping service for international returns to ensure your package arrives safely.

Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. These are typically clearance items, custom orders, or deeply discounted pieces. All Final Sale items are clearly marked on product pages.

Damaged or Defective Items

While rare, if you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@atelier-fabrics.com with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a free return and send you a replacement or full refund, including original shipping costs.

Please inspect your order upon arrival. Claims for damaged items must be made within 48 hours of delivery.

Lost Packages

If your package shows as delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbors and your building's front desk. If you still can't locate it after 48 hours, contact us and we'll file a claim with the shipping carrier.

Store Credit

If you'd prefer, we can issue returns as store credit instead of a refund to your original payment method. Store credit never expires and can be used on any future purchase, including sale items.

Questions About Returns?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm EST. We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
