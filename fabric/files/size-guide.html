How to Measure

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground. The tape should be snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is typically the narrowest part of your torso, about an inch above your belly button.

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.

Sleeve Length

Measure from the center back of your neck, across your shoulder, and down to your wrist bone with your arm slightly bent.

Inseam

Measure from the top of your inner thigh down to the bottom of your ankle. Best done with a pair of well-fitting pants.

Rise

Measure from the center front waistband down through the legs to the center back waistband. Determines how high or low pants sit.
